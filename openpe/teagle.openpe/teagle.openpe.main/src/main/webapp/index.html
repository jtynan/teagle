Open Policy Engine

Open Mobile Alliance Service Environment (OSE) compliant Policy Enforcer


The Open Policy Engine(OpenPE) has been developed as a consequence of the increased necessity of the telcom world for a system that provides a cost effectively and scalable management of the current resources and the future ones. It has been designed as a policy based service broker for architectures based on the nowadays SOA systems requirements, thus using the mechanism provided by the policy usage personalised services can be provided to end users and new services can be build upon the existing ones or the ones provided by third party companies.
